Mosaic teaches people — from 4th graders to adults in the workplace —
how to reach over the things that divide us in order to build inclusive communities where we celebrate our diversity.

In doing so, we transform our lives and our world. We make peace. Join us.

ASSERTIVENESS

“I’ve changed after Mosaic because I’m better at solving conflicts and problems that the world has. Also, I’m better at standing up for myself. I used to be really passive. I think I’ll keep those changes.”

— Tiana, Student

CONFLICT RESOLUTION

“I used to be a bystander, but now, after Mosaic, I know how to be an ally and help both the teaser and the person being teased.”

— Jack, student

EMPATHY

“I learned how awful it feels to not be listened to, to be excluded and discriminated against. These things will be taken with me as I step out of Mosaic and into my school.”

— Leah, student

TEAM BUILDING

— Leslie, teacher

DIVERSITY
